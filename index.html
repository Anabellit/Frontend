Live in another city and start your new life!

Sign up and start today!

How does it work?
  • Register and create a profile of your own home - for free! Give a comprehensive description of your home, your city and your neighborhood.
  • Upload photos of your home to give insights in what makes your home unique and special.
  • Go to our catalog and search for accommodation. You can search by city name and filter by country and type of house.
  • If you found what you're looking for, send a request to swap and introduce yourself.
  • If your request gets accepted: you can start your new life!
  • Should you not have found what you've been looking for, check back as new homes are uploaded every day!
